## **Section 1: Problem Agitation & Awareness (Questions 1-4)**
*Goal: Make them aware of the problem and feel the urgency*

**"How familiar are you with AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google's AI Overviews?"**

## **Scoring & Segmentation Logic**

### High-Priority Lead (Immediate Follow-up)
- Aware of AI search (Q1: Very/Somewhat familiar)
- Knows competitors are ahead (Q3: Yes, losing deals)
- Urgent timeline (Q6: Immediately/1-3 months)
- Budget >$10K/month (Q8)
- Decision maker (Q12: Final decision maker)

**Action: Book discovery call immediately**

---

### Qualified Lead (Nurture with Content)
- Moderately aware (Q1: Somewhat/Heard of)
- Suspects problem (Q3: I suspect)
- 3-6 month timeline (Q6)
- Budget $5K-$25K (Q8)
- Influencer (Q12: Key influencer)

**Action: Send case studies, invite to webinar**

---

### Education Lead (Long-term Nurture)
- Low awareness (Q1: Not familiar)
- No urgency (Q6: Within a year)
- Budget uncertain (Q8: Still determining)
- Researching (Q12: Gathering info)

**Action: Email course, blog content, quarterly check-ins**
